Brand Voice

Art.com is your friendly and knowledgable art confidan.

The conversational tone of Art.com immediately establishes a collaborative rapport with our customers. Never condescending or exclusive, the language of Art.com is friendly, inspirational, and insightful. Using the first person plural (e.g."We"), our tone is knowledgeable but approachable.
